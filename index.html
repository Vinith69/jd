About Us

At JD Associates, we are passionate about creating spaces that inspire and elevate lifestyles. Based in Udupi and catering to the entire coastal Karnataka region, we are a leading real estate aggregator with a proven track record in delivering residential layouts, housing colonies, and individual homes.

With a strong commitment to affordability and uncompromising quality, our projects are designed to meet the needs of modern families without stretching their budgets. Our experienced team ensures timely delivery, strategic locations, and customer-centric services, making us a trusted name in real estate.
